# Closed Circuit 

Go to work. Eat. Go to sleep. Wake up. Repeat.

## Help

CC is a text based adventure game. To play you just type commands to 
your character. Here are all the basic commands:

#### Movement

- Go

- Climb

- Hide

- Wait

- Sneak

#### Combat

- Hit

- Kick

- Knock

#### Interaction with items

- Pick up

- Eat

- Use

- Inspect

- Equip

#### Interaction with people

- Speak

- Whisper

- What can I say?

#### Special

- Play dead

- Dance
